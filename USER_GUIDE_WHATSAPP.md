# WhatsApp Connection - User Guide

## 📱 How to Connect Your WhatsApp

### Step 1: Access the Connection Page

1. Log into your account at the campaign sender dashboard
2. Click on **"WhatsApp Connection"** in the header navigation
3. You'll be taken to the WhatsApp connection page

### Step 2: Check Your Connection Status

When you arrive at the page, you'll see one of two states:

#### ✅ Already Connected
If your WhatsApp is already connected, you'll see:
- A green checkmark
- "WhatsApp Connected!"
- Your device name (e.g., "Asys Tvl")
- Your phone number (e.g., "+91 90254 00934")
- A button to go back to the dashboard

**You're all set! No action needed.**

#### ⚪ Not Connected
If your WhatsApp is not connected, you'll see:
- A gray icon
- "WhatsApp Not Connected"
- A big green button: **"Generate QR Code"**

**Continue to Step 3 below.**

### Step 3: Generate QR Code

1. Click the **"Generate QR Code"** button
2. Wait a few seconds while the QR code is generated
3. A large QR code will appear on your screen

### Step 4: Scan the QR Code

Now, grab your phone and follow these steps:

#### On iPhone:
1. Open **WhatsApp** on your iPhone
2. Tap **Settings** (bottom right)
3. Tap **Linked Devices**
4. Tap **Link a Device**
5. Point your camera at the QR code on the screen
6. Wait for the connection to complete

#### On Android:
1. Open **WhatsApp** on your Android phone
2. Tap the **three dots** (top right)
3. Tap **Linked devices**
4. Tap **Link a device**
5. Point your camera at the QR code on the screen
6. Wait for the connection to complete

### Step 5: Wait for Confirmation

- After scanning, the page will **automatically detect** the connection
- You'll see a green checkmark and "WhatsApp Connected!" message
- Your device name and phone number will be displayed
- **No need to refresh** - the page updates automatically!

## ⏱️ How Long Does It Take?

- **QR Generation**: 2-5 seconds
- **Scanning**: Instant
- **Connection Detection**: 5-10 seconds (automatic)

The page checks your connection status every 5 seconds, so you'll see the confirmation quickly.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Why do I need to connect WhatsApp?

Connecting your WhatsApp allows you to send campaign messages to your contacts directly from this platform. Without a connection, you cannot send messages.

### Is this safe?

Yes! This is the standard WhatsApp Web/Desktop connection method. You're simply linking this campaign tool as a connected device, just like WhatsApp Web.

### Can I disconnect later?

Yes! You can disconnect this device anytime from your phone:
1. Open WhatsApp on your phone
2. Go to Settings → Linked devices
3. Find this connection and tap "Log out"

### How many devices can I link?

WhatsApp allows up to 4 linked devices at once.

### What happens if I scan the wrong QR code?

Each QR code is unique to your account and session. If you accidentally scan a QR code from someone else's screen, their WhatsApp will be connected instead of yours. Always ensure you're scanning your own QR code.

### The QR code expired. What do I do?

QR codes expire after about 60 seconds for security. Simply click **"Generate New QR Code"** to get a fresh one.

### My phone can't scan the QR code. Help!

Try these steps:
1. Make sure your phone's WhatsApp is updated to the latest version
2. Check your internet connection on both phone and computer
3. Make sure the QR code is clearly visible (not too bright or dark)
4. Try generating a new QR code
5. Make sure your phone's camera can focus on the screen

### I scanned the code but it's not connecting

If it's been more than 30 seconds:
1. Check your phone has internet connection
2. Make sure WhatsApp is open on your phone
3. The page should automatically detect the connection within 5-10 seconds
4. Try refreshing the page and starting over

### Can I use this on multiple devices?

You can connect from different browsers or computers, but each connection will show as a separate linked device in WhatsApp.

## 🚨 Troubleshooting

### Issue: "Could not generate QR code"

**What to do:**
1. Refresh the page
2. Try again
3. If it still fails, contact support

### Issue: "Could not fetch connection status"

**What to do:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. If problem persists, contact support

### Issue: Page says "Unauthorized"

**What to do:**
1. You've been logged out
2. Go to the login page
3. Log in again
4. Return to the WhatsApp Connection page

### Issue: QR code is blurry or unclear

**What to do:**
1. Try zooming in on the page (Ctrl/Cmd + +)
2. Generate a new QR code
3. Adjust your screen brightness
4. Try a different browser

## ✅ Best Practices

### Keep Your Connection Active
- Your WhatsApp must remain connected to send campaigns
- Check the connection page occasionally to ensure you're still connected
- If you see "Disconnected", reconnect using the QR code

### Phone Battery
- Keep your phone charged
- WhatsApp on your phone must be active for the connection to work

### Internet Connection
- Both your computer and phone need internet
- Stable Wi-Fi is recommended for best results

### Security
- Never scan QR codes from untrusted sources
- Only scan QR codes from your own campaign dashboard
- Log out from Settings → Linked devices if you want to disconnect
